Keto

  • High fat, moderate protein, low carbohydrate.
  • Typically 20-50 grams carbs per day.

Ketovore

  • Carnivore with a small allowance for any low-carb foods.
  • Generally less than 10 grams carbs per day.

Carnivore

  • Exclusively animal products.
  • Zero carbs, as the diet eliminates all plant-based foods.

Meatan

  • A meatan is a person who follows a carnivore diet.
  • "I don't eat what my food eats".
